DJ A4

Superstart DJ A4, Boom is a DJ specializing in sounds of the Afrobeats but not only limited to Afrobeats, he's so good that he can blend in almost any genre of music. He like amapiano too, na chief cook for that part..

Engineer by day

At the age of 16, DJ A4 got his first job as a DJ begin in the hood, poplularly known as trenches., where he honed his skills and developed his signature style of mixing and blending different genres of music. He quickly gained a following among local listeners, who tuned in to hear his eclectic mixes and infectious energy.

After several years of working at the radio station, DJ A4 decided to take his talents to the club scene. He started performing at small local venues, gradually building his reputation as a skilled and versatile DJ. His ability to read a crowd and keep them dancing all night long soon caught the attention of larger clubs and festivals.
